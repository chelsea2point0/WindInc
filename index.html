Wind in the Forecast

Wind has been crafting hand-made sailboats since 1910. Now Wind is using the technology of tomorrow to bring you a world-class mobile app to encourage smooth sailing. The weather feature in the new Wind App will give you updates for impending storms or heavy winds in your area, so that you can be prepared for what's ahead.

Navigation

With the new Wind Navigation technology, you will always reach your intended destination without unnecessary detours. Save time and frustration and enter your destination via address or GPS coordinates, and the app will provide you the quickest and safest route available.
